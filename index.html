Get Started

High Aim Training Centre is a skills training centre.

The centre was set up in January 2021 by a group of specialists in skills and technical training who have since then been running it with the help of the local skilled staff

  • The High Aim Training Centre Management is composed of a team of directors who are the specialist in skills and technical training
  • We value ourselves as leaders in the provision of the best education to the youths, the poor and the disadvantaged
  • We strive to provide Total Quality Services

The Centre was established in order to reassure youths who have lost hope of advancing further in life by equipping them with skill in Plumbing and pipe fitting, Computer studies, Welding and Metal fabrication, Driving school, Food production and General Hospitality.

Courses

Popular Courses

Computer Studies

Computer studies is a course that covers a broad range of topics related to computers, technology, and programming. It is designed to provide students with the skills and knowledge needed to operate, troubleshoot and maintain computer systems.

Food production and general Hospitality

This course is designed to provide students with the skills and knowledge needed to prepare, cook and serve food for a variety of events and occasions. The course covers a broad range of topics related to the catering industry, including menu planning, food safety and hygiene, kitchen management, nutrition, and customer service. Students learn about different cooking techniques and methods, including baking, roasting, grilling, and frying, and how to create dishes that meet specific dietary requirements.

Welding and metal fabrication

This course is designed to provide students with the skills and knowledge needed to work with metal, including cutting, shaping, and joining metal components. The course covers a broad range of topics related to welding and metal fabrication, including welding techniques, metallurgy, blueprint reading, and safety procedures.
